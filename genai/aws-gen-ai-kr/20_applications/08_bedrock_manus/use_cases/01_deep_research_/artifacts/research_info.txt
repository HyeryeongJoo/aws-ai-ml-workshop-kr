## Problem Statement
Research current AWS cloud market status and trends in Korea, focusing on market share, revenue, key industries, competitors, and growth projections for 2025-2030.

## Research Findings

### Topic 1: Current AWS Cloud Market Status in Korea
- As of 2023, the overall Korean cloud market reached 7.39 trillion won, showing a 27% growth from 5.84 trillion won in 2022 [1]
- AWS dominates the Korean cloud service market with approximately 60% market share [2]
- In global context, AWS maintains 32-33% market share in Q2 2024 [3]
- Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) and Software as a Service (SaaS) account for 78% of total cloud market revenue in Korea [1]
- IaaS segment showed 24% growth reaching 3.17 trillion won in 2023 [1]

## Original full text
[1]: The Korean cloud market size exceeded 7 trillion won. According to the '2024 Cloud Industry Survey Results Report' published by the Ministry of Science and ICT and NIPA, cloud industry sales in Korea reached 7.3954 trillion won in 2023, an increase of about 1.55 trillion won (27%) from 5.8410 trillion won in 2022. IaaS company sales increased by 24% from 2.5577 trillion won in 2022 to 3.1687 trillion won in 2023.

[2]: According to the '2023 Value-Added Telecommunications Business Survey' released by the Ministry of Science and ICT last year, Amazon Web Services (AWS) has a 60% market share in the domestic cloud service market.

[3]: In Q2 2024, Amazon AWS maintains about 32% of the cloud market share, continuing to maintain its position as market leader. This is followed by Microsoft Azure (23%) and Google.

- Cloud adoption acceleration in financial and public sectors, with financial cloud market growing 32% in 2023 [12]

### Topic 6: Challenges and Obstacles for AWS in Korea
- Supply constraints limiting growth potential, with demand currently exceeding available capacity [14]
- Intense competition from local providers in regulated industries (public sector, government, finance) [15]
- Need for significant infrastructure investments to meet growing demand [14]
- Competition from other global providers, particularly Microsoft Azure's rapid growth in AI services [11]

### Topic 7: Regulatory Environment for Cloud Services
- Cloud Computing Development and User Protection Act governs cloud services in Korea [16]
- AWS maintains comprehensive compliance with Korean regulations through:
  * Regular third-party validations for security and compliance standards [17]
  * Shared responsibility model for security and compliance [18]
  * Support for local data sovereignty requirements [17]
- Financial sector regulations requiring special attention to data security and privacy [17]

### Topic 8: AWS Partnerships and Initiatives in Korea
- Strategic focus on AI infrastructure development through partnerships [19]
- Integration of new AI models like DeepSeek R1 into AWS platform [11]
- Expansion of data center capacity and infrastructure investments [13]
- Partnerships with financial institutions and public sector organizations [12]

### Topic 9: Expert Opinions on Future Prospects
